The Mechanism

1. Deep Research

We do not guess. Cold AI agents scrape and analyse live data points (LinkedIn profiles, posts, company news) to construct a mental model of your prospect.

2. Intelligent Drafting

The engine uses this research to draft high-context messages. It avoids generic fluff, referencing specific pain points found in the research.

3. Human-in-the-Loop (HITL)

The Safety Layer. Pure automation is dangerous. Cold AI queues every message for review. You approve, edit, or reject. We scale your outreach without losing the human touch.
