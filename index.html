Cold AI

Intelligent outreach engine for LinkedIn and Email. Automated research and drafting with mandatory human sign-off.

Core Concept

Cold AI solves the "spam vs scale" dilemma. It automates the heavy lifting of prospecting—researching targets and drafting context-aware messages—but waits for human approval before sending.

This "human-in-the-loop" architecture ensures brand safety while allowing teams to scale personalized outreach.

Functionality

1. Research. The engine scans live data sources (news, social activity, funding rounds) to find relevant hooks.

2. Drafting. It constructs messages using your tone of voice, referencing the specific research findings.

3. Review. Messages sit in a queue. You approve, edit, or reject. The system learns from your edits.

Ecosystem

Built API-first for agentic workflows. Integrates seamlessly with Warm AI to capture and convert inbound traffic.
