About Cold AI

We are building the autonomous revenue infrastructure for the next decade of B2B sales. Based in Guildford, United Kingdom.

The Philosophy

Smarter, Not Louder

We believe outbound sales is broken. It has become a race to the bottom, dominated by spam and volume. The solution is not just "more automation," but "intelligent automation."

Agentic Workflows

Traditional tools just send templates. Cold AI deploys autonomous agents that research the prospect first, understanding context and relevance before a single message is drafted.

The Goal

To give sales teams 100x leverage. One person should be able to run a hyper-personalised campaign that previously required a team of ten SDRs.
