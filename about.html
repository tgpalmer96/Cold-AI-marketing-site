About

Cold AI is based in London. We are building the outreach engine for the next decade of B2B sales.

The Philosophy

We believe outbound sales is broken. It has become a race to the bottom, dominated by spam and volume. The solution is not "more automation," but "smarter automation."

We view AI as a force multiplier, not a replacement. Our technology handles the tedious work (research and drafting), but we enforce a human-in-the-loop workflow to ensure quality never drops.

The Goal

To give sales teams 100x leverage. One person should be able to run a hyper-personalised campaign that previously required a team of ten SDRs.
