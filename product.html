The Engine

Cold AI is not a template tool. It is an agentic workflow that mimics the behaviour of an elite SDR.

1. Live Data Acquisition

Unlike database tools that rely on stale data, Cold AI utilizes enterprise-grade APIs to access live profile information. We analyse:

  • Profile Data: Job history, skills, and bio context.
  • Activity Signals: Recent posts, comments, and shared content.
  • Company Context: Hiring trends and recent news.

2. Drafting & Synthesis

Our LLM pipeline synthesizes this data to create a "Hook." The message is drafted to sound like you—direct, low-friction, and relevant.

3. The Sign-Off Interface

Brand Safety

Your reputation is the asset. Cold AI creates a queue of drafts. A human reviewer can scan, edit, and click "Approve" in seconds. This allows one person to manage the volume of 10 SDRs while maintaining 100% quality control.

Compliance & Security

Cold AI is GDPR compliant and built on secure, enterprise-grade infrastructure. We process business data for legitimate interest and retain strict data minimization policies.

Warm AI (Integration)

Warm AI identifies companies visiting your website. It feeds these high-intent signals directly into Cold AI, allowing you to trigger outbound sequences to companies that are already researching you.
