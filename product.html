The Engine

Automate your outreach without losing the human touch.

1. Live Intelligence

Database tools rely on old info. Cold AI looks at what your prospect is doing right now.

  • Profile Data: We analyze their career history and skills.
  • Recent Activity: We see their posts, comments, and shares.
  • Company News: We track funding rounds and hiring surges.

2. Smart Drafting

Most AI sounds like a robot. Cold AI is different. It uses the research to write a natural, relevant message that fits your prospect's context perfectly. It's not a template—it's a conversation starter.

3. The Control Layer

Executive Sign-Off

Automation can be dangerous. Cold AI solves this by queuing every draft for your review. You can scan 50 messages in minutes, approve the good ones, and reject the bad ones. You get 10x the volume with 100% safety.
